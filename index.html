Who We Are

Your Construction Intelligent Partner

Squnga’esihle Trading (Pty) Ltd is a project management, social facilitation and housing development enterprise built for impact — delivering end-to-end solutions across the urban and rural housing sector of KwaZulu-Natal. We understand that true development goes beyond bricks and mortar; it is about people, communities, and the futures we collectively build. Every project we touch represents a community served, a family empowered, and a municipality transformed.

Core Service Offerings

Project Management
  • Project start-up meetings with clients
  • Procurement & management of service providers
  • Project kick-off & technical meetings

  • Completion of subsidy administration forms

  • SPLUMA submissions & site verification
